An unlawful arrest violates your fundamental constitutional rights. If you or a loved one has been arrested without lawful cause, detained unlawfully, or subjected to police misconduct, you may have a claim for damages against the arresting authority or the South African Police Service.

Our attorneys are experienced in pursuing constitutional damages claims and Section 342A applications against the State — holding law enforcement accountable and securing appropriate compensation for our clients.

Key Services

  • Wrongful Arrest Claims
  • Unlawful Detention
  • Police Brutality & Misconduct
  • Constitutional Damages Claims
  • Malicious Prosecution
  • Claims Against the SAPS
  • Section 35 Constitutional Rights
  • Urgent Release Applications

Administering a deceased estate can be a complex and emotionally taxing process. Our team assists executors, heirs, and next-of-kin in navigating the estate administration process under the Administration of Estates Act — from reporting the estate to the Master of the High Court through to final distribution to beneficiaries.

We act as professional executors and advisors in both testate (with a will) and intestate (without a will) estates, ensuring that assets are properly identified, valued, and distributed in accordance with the law and the wishes of the deceased.

  • Asset Identification & Valuation

  • Beneficiary Disputes
  • Estate Duty Compliance
