The South African labour environment is heavily regulated and rapidly evolving. Our labour law practice advises both employers and employees on the full range of employment-related matters — from day-to-day HR legal compliance through to complex retrenchment processes and appellate proceedings before the Labour Appeal Court.

We represent clients at the CCMA, Bargaining Councils, Labour Court, and Labour Appeal Court, with a strong track record in both claimant and respondent matters.

Key Services

  • Employment Contract Drafting
  • Unfair Dismissal & Discrimination
  • CCMA Representation
  • Retrenchments & Section 189 Process
  • Disciplinary Hearings
  • Collective Bargaining
  • Strike & Lockout Advisory
  • HR Policy Development
